Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and gooey cheese, all baked to perfection.

The combination of savory flavors and soft, chewy textures makes lasagna a family favorite, ideal for gatherings or a cozy night in.

Ingredients Required

For the meat sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps to Cook

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef or sausage, cooking until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and red wine (if using). Season with basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce on low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  6. Add the lasagna noodles and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  7. Drain the noodles and lay them flat on a clean kitchen towel to prevent sticking.
  8. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well until smooth.
  9. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  10. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  11. Place 3-4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, overlapping slightly.
  12. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  13. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella, until all the ingredients are used. Finish with a final layer of meat sauce and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  14. Cover the lasagna with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  16. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing.
  17. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and serve with a side of garlic bread or salad.
Enjoy your Lasagna!
